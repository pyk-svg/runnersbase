The Runner's Base

Runner’s Base is a complete beginner-to-intermediate guide aimed at students who are past ‘just starting out’ but don’t have a clear next step: most intermediate guides online are written for adults, whose needs differ from those of a teen runner’s. Runner’s Base instead explains how to train effectively, even at a casual level; how to scale training, even with schoolwork; and everything in-between PE & racing to get prospective runners on the line. This is all explained in a language that doesn’t assume you already know the jargon, reinforced by interactive tools to use concepts you learn about with just a click of a button (pace calculator, training plan generator) and written by someone who has gone through this process themselves (2x SEASAC racer.)
